# Power Systems — How Abilities Work in the World

All power in this world is **nanite-mediated**. During the Transition, ambient nanite fields were bound to philosophical disciplines — the shape of your training determines what the field will do for you. It responds to practiced intent, not wishes.

## Rules the narration must respect
1. **Distance matters.** Power weakens away from its civilization's power center (inverse-square feel). A Harmonic adept is formidable on the Heights, capable in the valley, and struggling deep in Radiant territory.
2. **Interference is real.** Where fields overlap (the Disputed Zone), effects distort unpredictably: sound bends light, light carries sound, small time-pockets form. Techniques there can fail strangely or succeed impossibly.
3. **Cost is real.** Every technique draws personal energy. Exhausted users get feedback: headaches, nosebleeds, resonance-burn or light-scald. Nobody casts all day.
4. **Skill is earned.** Levels: Initiate → Apprentice → Adept → Master → Grandmaster. An Initiate moving a cup is honest work. Masters tune weather. Grandmasters are history-book figures.
5. **Combination is rare and potent.** Cross-system technique (sonic + photonic together) is barely understood, culturally fraught, and the likely key to the valley's mysteries.

## The two local systems
- **Harmonic (sonic)**: resonance, vibration, echo-perception, standing-wave barriers, calming or shattering tones. Aesthetic: felt pressure, visible ripples, choral overtones.
- **Radiant (photonic)**: light-bending, spectra-sight, coherent beams, mirage and revelation. Aesthetic: shimmer, prism-flare, clean silent precision.

## The wider world (rumored, distant)
Beyond the mountains, five great civilizations shape whole climates with their own systems: Greek natural forces, Roman structured order, Hindu cyclical powers, Japanese harmonic balance, and the Quantum Void enclaves of the poles. To valley folk these are traveler's tales. The mountain pass just made them real.
