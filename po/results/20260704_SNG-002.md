## What shipped

- Rules: `encounters` block in resolution.json — duel margin table (crit −3/succ −2/partial −1&−1/fail −2p/critfail −3p hits, playerHealthPerHit 4, threat×0.3→difficulty, 3 energy/round, flee-fail free hit), challenge failure costs (3hp/5en/1h default, ×2 crit), puzzle costs (4en/1h per attempt), outcome XP (win 15/complete 12/solve 15/flee 5/abandon 3/walk-away 2).
- Content: `zone_raider_duel` (Vess, threat 45, 5 hits, yields at 1), `rockslide_crossing` (3 stages with per-stage failure costs), `precursor_mechanism` (diff 30, 3 hint tiers, 2 codex unlocks incl. the-gray-water at −15; GM-eyes-only truth: watershed maintenance records — major crisis lead). Manifest + encounterSeeds on 3 locations.
- GM: AVAILABLE ENCOUNTERS + ACTIVE ENCOUNTER context blocks; choices carry `encounterId`; `encounterOps` clamped to opponent tacticTags (flavor-only); **rule 18 drafted and live — see below, awaiting Erik ratification**.
- UI: encounter bar (name/round/both-sides status; puzzle hints revealed by max(sense tier, partials)); guaranteed action buttons (flee/yield · stage/abandon · attempt/unlock/walk-away); danger styling.

## Verification (per criteria)
1. **180/180 smoke tests** — 31 new: every transition (start×3+invalid, difficulty mapping ×3, duel margins/yield/fall/flee-both/yield-op/incapacitation, challenge succ/partial/fail/crit-double/complete/abandon, puzzle hint-gating/partial-reveal/fail/solve/walk-away/codex-unlock, receipt guardrail text, ops clamped, tactic flavor-only).
2. **Duel live**: scripted 3-round win (receipts visible: opp 5→3→2→0, player −4 on partial) + flee run (fail = free hit taken, then fled). Real GM narration of the final receipt: names Vess, uses his tacticTags, honors the Zone. Two end states reached (opponent_fell, fled).
3. **Challenge**: engine-verified — partial applies stage cost AND advances; failure holds with retry; completion at final stage.
4. **Puzzle**: hints gate 0/2/3 by sense tier; codex unlock appears only with the-gray-water known (smoke-verified).
5. **Degradation**: state moves engine-side BEFORE the GM call by construction; GM parse failure → salvage/prose fallback (v1.0.1 path, unit-tested) leaves encounter state intact and next round playable.
6. **Rule 18 draft** (LIVE in GM_SYSTEM, flagged for ratification): *"ENCOUNTERS (duels, challenges, puzzles) are typed engine structures. When AVAILABLE ENCOUNTERS lists one and the fiction invites it, offer it as a choice carrying its exact encounterId — never invent encounter ids. During an ACTIVE ENCOUNTER: the receipt block is the complete mechanical truth of the round — narrate it vividly and exactly; never move health, stages, hints, or outcomes yourself. Offer round choices that fit (press, defend, trick, ability or item use, and where sensible flee/yield/abandon). Your only mechanical lever is encounterOps op 'tactic' (an opponent flavor tag from their tacticTags). Duels end in yields, falls, or flights — narrate defeat as incapacitation and consequence, never death."*
