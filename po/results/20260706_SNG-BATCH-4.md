## SNG-010C — Item evolution (Waystaff wakes through Aevi): SHIPPED (v1.6.5)
- **engine/evolution.js** — an item may carry `evolution{bondSource, coUseTag, stages[]}`. A stage unlocks only when BOTH the linked companion's bond ≥ `stage.unlockBond` AND the item×companion co-activation ≥ `stage.unlockCoUse`. Co-use is tallied in the practice ledger under a namespaced `coUse[itemId+companionId]` key every time the player channels a cast with that companion present.
- **Save-light:** stage *definitions* stay in the CONTENT catalog (never copied into saves); the save holds only the co-use tally + the current `evoStage` stamp. Migration refreshes stages on load; non-evolving items are untouched (verified).
- **Grants apply additively:** a stage shift updates the item's effective `bonusTags` and gives an escalating focus bonus via `inventory.equipmentBonus` (`evoStageStep`) — the staff helps with more kinds of casts and more strongly as it wakes. GM gets a LIVING GEAR block (current stage + foreshadow of the next) every turn and an AN ITEM WAKES block on advance; a ✦ aside marks the moment for the player.
- Smoke: gates on BOTH bond and co-use (neither alone advances); stage grant/bonusTags apply; cast-with-companion advances; non-bonded/non-evolving items unaffected; GM told the stage.

## SNG-015 — Variable-power ability use + action customization: SHIPPED (v1.6.6)
- **Part A — mechanic (engine/intensity.js):** an ability use resolves at Conserve / Standard / Surge. Energy scales (0.6 / 1.0 / 1.6× with content floors — conserve ≥ 2, surge ≤ 2× standard); effect scales (effectMod folded into the roll); **Surge alone carries backlash**, paid by the ability's Tier (`surgeBacklashByTier`) when a surged roll slips (chance rises on marginal/failed, near-nil on a clean success). **AUTO** picks the minimum intensity the task needs via the standard-chance comfort read and **NEVER auto-surges**. Intensity scales roll/effect + energy only — it never bypasses attribute gates or levelReq (a use only ever involves an already-owned ability). Roll receipt shows intensity + energy spent + surge backlash; GM told the intensity to narrate to it.
- **Part B — action customization (UI):** every ability-capable choice is **tap-to-commit** (auto intensity + the choice's default ability) OR **⚙ expand-to-tune** — an inline panel exposing which owned ability to apply + a Conserve/Standard/Surge dial showing each step's energy cost and a Surge backlash warning. Fast path stays one tap; depth is one expand.
- **GM contract (minor — flag for Erik):** narrate to the chosen intensity (a conserved cast reads restrained; a surge reads as pushing hard; a backlash as real cost). Intensity/backlash are engine-computed and clamped — GM narrates, never sets them. Design law 1 intact.
- Smoke: resolves at each step with correct energy/effect; auto picks conserve on easy + never surges at any difficulty; surge backlash scales by tier + only fires on a surge; dial exposes all three steps with scaled energy + surge warning; gates/levelReq untouched by intensity.
