# Results — SNG-048 (Narrative register keyed to disposition + rating)

Date: 2026-07-11 · Shipped v1.8.5 · 696 smoke checks (+7) + parse_probe green · boots clean; register verified in-browser. Status: **shipped, awaiting Erik preview-legs before complete_pending_review**. Only Aevi closes.

**The "GM over-writes abstractly everywhere / R+ chokes on intimacy" fix.** Register is now `f(disposition, rating)` — disposition sets the poetry, rating sets the heat — both engine-computed from data the world already carries.

## Lever 1 — disposition (concrete ↔ poetic)
The world already has a `concrete_abstract` spectrum axis; register IS that axis expressing through prose. `narrativeRegister(location)` (pure, in `gm.js`) computes a band from the axis + overall pole **charge**:
- **concrete-leaning / low-charge / ordinary place → CONCRETE** (the default): plain, sensory-literal — what's actually there, in first-read words. No abstract personification, no "soil's long exhale" on a road.
- **abstract-pole + charged (sacred/temporal/precursor/strange) → the register EARNS the poetic/lyrical** — that voice belongs HERE, not everywhere.
- **middle band → mostly concrete**, at most one image carrying the odd charge.
- **Axis-flavored tint** (core lever stays concrete_abstract + charge): truth-charged → stark/clear; falsehood → slippery; spiritual → lyrical, mechanical → clinical-strange; life → verdant, death → hushed.
- **Rule 5 rewritten** to default hard toward concrete and escalate only as much as the register cue says the place earned. Fed to the GM as a `## NARRATIVE REGISTER` world block.

## Lever 2 — rating as DIRECTION (not just a cap)
Diagnosis (from the spec, confirmed): the rating reached the GM only as a CEILING (a cap), never a direction — so R+ was *permitted* up to R+ but never *directed* to write the full mature register, and collapsed intimacy to PG. `ratingRegister(preset)` (pure) now gives the **affirmative register per ceiling**, folded into the CONTENT CEILING line:
- **G/PG** chaste, gentle; violence bloodless; closeness is warmth.
- **PG-13/R** real stakes; violence has blood; intimacy carries heat and tension.
- **R+** the FULL mature register — visceral on violence/injury/aftermath; charged, sensual, evocative on intimacy and desire (heat, breath, tension, touch, longing); vivid, adult, unhurried. **Evocative, NOT explicit depiction of sexual acts** — a charged erotic REGISTER, never graphic mechanics. Directed not to collapse an intimate/violent beat to a milder one.
- **Both floors unchanged + absolute:** minor-protection (a minor is never in romantic/sexual framing for any viewer) and no-prohibited-content stay hard, independent of ceiling. R+ raises the register toward the floor; it never touches it.

## Guardrails held
Engine computes the register cue from existing spectrum/poleIntensity + the profile ceiling (no new data); GM narrates to it (contract change, no mechanic change); default concrete unless the place earns otherwise; floors absolute + independent of the register. Design law 1 intact. Never touched the ErikIAm pipeline.
