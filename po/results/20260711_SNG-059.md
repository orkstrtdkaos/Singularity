# Results — SNG-059 Character creation v2 (⭐ priority — Brooklyn's builder)

Date: 2026-07-11 · Shipped v1.8.18 · 759 smoke checks (+13 domain-model) + parse_probe green · **live-verified end-to-end on a fresh port**. Status: **shipped, awaiting Erik/Brooklyn preview-legs before complete_pending_review**. Only Aevi closes.

The most out-of-date surface in the game — the character builder — now carries all the new content, shipped as **one coherent creation flow** (not four tickets).

## 1. Domain choice on THE GREAT CIRCLE (SNG-055)
- New pure module **`engine/traditions.js`** — the entire geometry is **read from `content/packs/core/rules/traditions.json`**, never hardcoded: `buildTraditionIndex`, `ringDistance` (`min(|i−j|, 24−|i−j|)`), `antipodeOf`, `neighborsOf`, `ringOrder`, `domainAccess`, `inferDomains`. Loaded into `CONTENT.traditions` + `CONTENT.traditionIndex`.
- **The picker** renders the 24 traditions as a ring; you choose **primary → secondary → tertiary** in sequence. **Tertiary is constrained to a ring-neighbour of the secondary.** The **antipode** of any chosen pole is drawn **CLOSED** (red, unpickable), with the axis line shown — you can't hold both ends of a tension you've chosen.
- **`domainAccess`** (the gate): primary = full/no penalty · adjacent-to-primary = free except capstones (IV–V) · secondary = to tier III · tertiary = to tier II · far/adjacent-to-chosen = allowed with a skill-point penalty · **antipode = closed** · folk traditions = open. 13 unit tests pass against the real content.

## 2. Companion choice + naming (SNG-057)
- Creation offers the **9 authored companions** (aevi, bristle, ember, quill, tal, coil, hush, marrow, sprig) with name/role/appearance; you pick one and may **name** them. **Aevi is no longer the silent default.**
- Shape: `character.companions` stays `[id]` (recruitment/backfill compatible); `character.companionNames{id:name}` holds the player's name. `activeCompanions` overlays the chosen name so the **GM + portraits use it** (a renamed companion still generates from its authored `appearance`). A **✎ rename** affordance is on the companions panel in play too.

## 3. Form / appearance in creation (SNG-053)
- The SNG-053 form editor is surfaced **as a creation step**, so the portrait is right from the first render — an Ent is never born human.

## 4. Skill grouping by `tradition` (SNG-054/055)
- The **learn screen**, the **owned-abilities list**, and the **skill-graph columns + colors** now group by **`ability.tradition`** (the people), not `powerSystem`/reach — **Erik's "why are body_mind skills still connected" fix**. Traditions get consistent per-people colors on the graph; the learn list is grouped by people's names ("Learn The Umbrals").
- The learn list **enforces the domain gate**: the antipode and over-tier picks simply **aren't offered**; far ones are labelled and cost more.

## Migration (mandatory — nobody loses a skill or a companion)
- **`reconcile` step v5** infers a legacy character's domains from the traditions of the abilities they already hold (most-held → primary, etc.); **already-owned abilities rank freely** (only *learning new* ones is gated), so out-of-domain holdings are **grandfathered**. Folk-only characters stay open. `companionNames` initialized. Existing companions untouched.

## ⚠️ Content wiring done by CCode to unblock (flag for Aevi)
The spec said "all content is authored and at origin," but two things weren't loading:
- **The valley manifest listed only `aevi.json`** — the 8 other companion files existed but weren't in `provides.companions`, so they never loaded. **CCode added all 9 to the manifest.**
- **The 4 original companions (aevi/bristle/ember/quill) lacked `startingOption`** — CCode added `startingOption: true` so the full roster of 9 is offered (matching the SNG-059 spec's roster list).
These are small content touches to make the authored feature work; **Aevi owns the content going forward.**
