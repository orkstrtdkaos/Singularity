Closes the gap Aevi named: ~26 authored lore files (~180KB) were **unreadable in-game**. There was a discovered Codex (what your character found) but no way to just *read about the world*.

## What shipped — an OPEN Library, distinct from the DISCOVERED Codex

A `📖 The Library` entry point in **two places**:
- **The roster** — reachable **before you ever play** (Erik's requirement).
- **The play sidebar** — a new "Codex & Library" section: "open Codex" (discovered, gated) + "The Library — read the world" (open).

The Codex is unchanged — it keeps its per-character discovery gate. The Library is the player's book of the world, always open.

### Four categories, authored lore as prose

### THE GREAT CIRCLE as a readable thing (not just a gate)
`libGreatCircle()` renders the shared `domainCircleSVG` (all 24 stations on the ring, read from `traditions.json` — **never hardcoded**) as a non-selectable centerpiece, then lists all **24 peoples** with pole, antipode (across the ring), civilization, and aesthetic, plus the **3 folk crafts** (open to all). This is the same ring the SNG-055/059 domain model uses, now legible.

### The renderers (generic, reusable)
- `loreToHtml(value, depth)` — walks any authored JSON into headings + prose: strings→`<p>`, string-arrays→`<ul>`, objects→titled sections, arrays-of-objects→sub-entries titled by name/title/label/people/craft/role.
- `libMdToHtml(md)` — headings/lists/paragraphs **plus inline `**bold**` / `*italic*` / `_italic_`** (`libInline` on already-escaped text — the primer uses bold heavily; without it, literal `**` leaked).
- `libFetch(path, kind)` — cached fetch (json or text).

## The hard constraint held: GM-EYES-ONLY never leaks
`libSkipKey(k)` drops two rings of keys from **every** render:
- **Meta:** `schemaVersion|id|kind|note|designNote|buildPlan|buildNeeds|owed|migration|status|version|packId`
- **Secret (GM-only):** `/(gm[_A-Z]|gmeyes|eyes.?only|secret|hidden|hook|mandate|internal|_pat|token|guidance)/i` → catches `gmGuidance`, `gmMandate`, `hiddenTruth`, `hook`, `hooks`.

**Verified:** the 10 Library-included files carry **zero** GM/secret keys and zero `GM-EYES-ONLY` markers (the files that DO carry them — `domain_detail`, `methods_and_tools`, `world_node_atlas`, `chronicle_played_gambits`, `the_living_world`, `will_modality` — are **not** in the Library index). The filter is defense-in-depth for any future field or index expansion.
