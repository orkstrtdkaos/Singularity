# Results — SNG-062 The Prologue (character creation as a played scene)

Date: 2026-07-11 · Shipped v1.8.19 · 764 smoke checks (+5) + parse_probe green · **live-verified full flow end-to-end on a fresh port**. Status: **shipped, awaiting Erik/Brooklyn preview-legs before complete_pending_review**. Only Aevi closes.

Character creation is now something that **happens to you**, not a form. Supersedes SNG-059's dropdown as the primary path (SNG-059's four components remain the requirements; the Prologue is the delivery). Absorbs SNG-039 onboarding.

## Two doors (parity mandatory)
At creation: **"▶ Play the opening (recommended)"** and **"⚡ Quick start (build it yourself)"**. The SNG-059 form is the **express lane** — same character shape, same domain count (3), same companion, no advantage either way. The Prologue *tells* you who you are; the form *asks*.

## The Prologue flow (all authored content — `content/packs/valley/prologue.json`; NO LLM, plays fully offline)
1. **Pick name + form only** (SNG-053 form leads the portrait — an Ent never arrives human).
2. **Choose an opening** — The Waystation (crisis/physical), The Thin Place (strange/precursor), The Debt (social/moral) — or **"Surprise me."**
3. **Play 4 problems.** Each carries 4 authored paths; every path has a `tradition` + `grantsAbility` + an authored `outcome`. The player just *acts* — nothing announces they're being measured.
4. **Skills come from what you USED** — the granted abilities become the starting kit ("you did this, so you know this"), never purchased.
5. **The companion ARRIVES in the scene** — an opening-specific offer (each opening's `companionBeat.offer`) with **per-companion arrival prose**; the player **chooses AND names** who stays. Met, never issued.
6. **Domains CRYSTALLIZE** from the accrued tradition-tags — new pure `traditions.crystallizeDomains`: heaviest tag = **primary**; **secondary** = 2nd-heaviest if within 3 ring-steps (never the antipode) else a ring-neighbour; **tertiary** = a ring-neighbour of the secondary. Shown on **THE GREAT CIRCLE** with the position lit, the **antipode drawn closed**, and the **reasons in the player's own actions** ("— Go in and get the dog. Now."). Then **ADJUST** (re-pick on the circle) and a **MANDATORY CONFIRM** — revealed, not imposed; the player keeps the last word.
7. **Out the far side:** a complete character — domains set, the abilities they *used*, a **named companion who joined for a reason**, a form, and a bio seeded from the opening.

## spec_boundary — starting-ability count
The Prologue grants **one ability per problem played (up to 4)** — the "skills from what you did" design. The quick-start form still grants the origin-based **1–2**. That's a **count mismatch** vs the "same starting-ability count" parity clause. Both are rank-1 abilities so the power gap is small, but the canonical starting-ability count for the two doors is a **design decision for Erik/Aevi to set** (make the form grant ~4 too, or cap the prologue). Flagged rather than silently changing the shipped form's count.
