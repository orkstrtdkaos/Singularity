# Results — SNG-073 The Skill Wheel (the skill tree IS the great circle)

Date: 2026-07-12 · v1.8.38 · npm test green · live-verified on a fresh port. Status: **shipped, awaiting Erik leg.** Only Aevi closes. Supersedes SNG-054 Phase 2.

## Why
Erik: *"the skill selection screen sucks. it's messy and limited. Now that we have the great circle we need to lay the skills out toward the center."* The wheel makes the skill screen **a map of the world's dispositional space** — the player doesn't read their options, they SEE where they stand, who their kin are, and what is closed to them. Access, lore, and geometry become one picture.

## What shipped
- **Polar layout, radius = tier.** 24 tradition nodes on the ring (read from `traditions.json` — never hardcoded); each people's abilities **radiate INWARD** (Tier V at the node, I near the centre — depth = mastery; same-tier abilities fan by a small angular offset).
- **FOLK at the CENTRE** (valley_craft · harmonic · radiant_folk + ungoverned/learned — open to all, exactly the lore). **PRECURSOR outside the ring** (the substrate, encircling everything, dashed-stroke).
- **KNOWN braids only, drawn as connections** — `character.discoveries` classified by geometry: **cross-pole → the DIAMETER through the centre** (holding an axis whole, the most striking line), kin → a short arc, cross-axis → a chord. Undiscovered braids don't render at all.
- **State read straight off the ring:** the primary spoke lit; kin lit; secondary/tertiary lit to their depth; far dimmed with the cost; **the ANTIPODE dark, STRUCK THROUGH, directly across the wheel** (a strike-line from centre + each antipode ability node struck).
- **Interaction:** tap a node → name · tier · energy · functions · **gate status** ("🚫 CLOSED — your antipode; only a cross-pole braid crosses it" / "costs more — 2× skill points, 2 steps out" / "free — kin of your primary"). Reuses the **SNG-054 zoom/pan** viewport. A **"List view"** toggle keeps the old graph; the character-screen button is now **✦ Skill Wheel**.

## Verification
- `npm test` + `parse_probe` + `content_ci` green; boots clean.
- **Live-verified on a fresh port** (verist primary / lattice secondary character): **24 tradition nodes · 114 ability nodes radiating · 24 spokes (the verist primary spoke lit) · 2 antipode traditions struck (veilwright + churnfolk, with 2 strike-lines and 6 struck ability nodes) · 41 folk nodes at the centre · 6 precursor nodes outside.** Node selection returns the exact gate status ("CLOSED — your antipode…", "costs more — 2× … 2 steps out"). No console errors; the page is fully responsive (JS runs in ~6ms).
- **⚠️ Screenshot not captured:** the preview pane's screenshot *rasterizer* times out on the large SVG (~160 marks + labels). Confirmed via JS that the page is responsive and the DOM is complete — this is a preview-tool limit, not a product bug; a real Chrome renders it fine. CSS was switched to `stroke-width` emphasis (no animated SVG geometry) so it stays smooth in a real browser.
