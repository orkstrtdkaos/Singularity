# Results — SNG-075 Encounters fire in narrative play

Date: 2026-07-12 · v1.8.28 · npm test green · boots clean on a fresh port. Status: **shipped, awaiting Erik leg.** Only Aevi closes.

## The gap
`maybeRandomEncounter` was called from **three UI-button paths only** (onTravel/onEnterLocation/onRest); the narrative turn loop had **zero** encounter hooks. 58 authored encounters + 22 regions of texture were effectively unreachable for a player who plays in prose and rarely touches the map. Not broken — **unhooked from how the game is actually played.**

## The fix — bind to narrative TIME, weave into the fiction
- **`engine/random_encounters.js`** (pure, tested): `narrativeTimeChance(hours, location, table)` — ~4%/hr, danger-weighted, clamped (~60% cap); `rollNarrativeTime`; `classifyNarrativeKind({intentTags, why, hoursPassed})` → `rest | travel | time | none` from the beat's elapsed fiction. Rates default in-engine (a mechanic, not content) but a pack MAY override via `triggerRules.onNarrativeTime {ratePerHour, maxChance}`.
- **`app.js` `maybeNarrativeEncounter`** (runs in `applyTurn`, after time advances): a 20-minute beat is ~1% (stays quiet), a half-day walk ~24%, a multi-day trek caps out. It **never fires a card** — it stashes the selected encounter's seed in `pendingWeave`.
- **The weave:** the next `runGM` reads `pendingWeave` and injects `encounterWeaveDetail` as authoritative context; `gm.js` renders a "## SOMETHING HAPPENS" scene block telling the GM to **weave it into the scene, no aside, no genre-break.** The road sounds like the road saying it.
- **The lethal rule holds on the narrative path:** if the encounter `canIncapacitate`, the weave text **mandates** a decline/flee/avoid choice before any engagement and forbids narrating the character as already fighting/harmed. No ambush-lethality.

## Suppression conditions `[CCODE]` (named per the spec)
No narrative encounter fires when: a live encounter is active · a gambit is in play (`gambitDraft`) · the scene just ended (`sceneEnded`) · one was already woven this scene (`sceneEncounterFired`, reset in `startScene`) · within a **3-turn cooldown** · the beat is intense/intimate (`sceneState.intense/intimate` or intent tags `intimate|climax|grief|vigil|mourn`). *A world that interrupts you constantly is as dead as one that never does.*

## Verification
- `npm test` green — 8 new checks: the ~1% / ~24% / capped / danger-nudge / zero-never-fires chance curve, and the rest/travel/time/none classifier.
- Fresh-port boot clean (new imports + the applyTurn hook + the runGM injection + the gm.js block).
- **Erik leg (needs a live GM):** "Play narratively — travel and camp by describing it, never touching the map — verify things happen: the road has something to say; a grace on a quiet day. Then have a short conversation — verify it stays quiet." The engine roll + weave wiring is proven; the GM actually narrating a woven encounter needs a live model.

## spec_boundaries
- **`onNarrativeTime` rates default in the engine** (0.04/hr, 0.6 cap) rather than in content, so SNG-075 ships without a content edit (content is Aevi's lane). If Aevi wants to tune the cadence, add `triggerRules.onNarrativeTime {ratePerHour, maxChance}` to `random_encounters.json` and the engine will use it.
- **Woven encounters run as fiction + normal skill-checks, not the mechanical duel offer-beat.** The full duel/challenge card stays the UI-travel path; the narrative path weaves the seed and the GM presents choices (engage → a normal risky check, or the mandated decline). This keeps "weave, don't interrupt" while honoring the lethal rule.
